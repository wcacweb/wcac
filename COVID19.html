親愛的弟兄姊妹，

您可能已經從新聞得知。最新的變種新冠病毒奧密克戎 (Omicron)在安省已經成為主要病 毒株。溫莎華人宣道會在此向我們的弟兄姊妹發布此建議藉以提醒弟兄姊妹保持警醒，保 護我們的家人及弟兄姊妹。

關於毒奧密克戎變種的一些資訊(資料來源美國疾病管制局 CDC):

  • 更容易傳播，傳染力很有可能比 Delta 變種更強。
  • 更多突破性感染
  • 主要的症狀
    1. 流鼻涕
    2. 頭痛
    3. 疲勞
    4. 打噴涕
    5. 喉嚨痛

給弟兄姊妹的建議

  • 請繼續確實遵守教會擬定的防疫措施包刮在教會全程戴口罩，保持社交安全距離及 注意各種新冠病毒病徵。
  • 如果您有外地來訪的客人或回家過節的家人，請特別注意有否以上形容的症狀。如 果同住的任何人有顯示以上的症狀，請留在家參加線上崇拜。
  • 請關注並遵守安省公布的任何新防疫及聚會限制措施。
  • 教會主日崇拜結束後，請不要逗留室內。如在停車場與弟兄姊妹交流，請繼續戴口罩及保持安全距離。

溫莎華人宣道會祝弟兄姊妹們有個安全平安的聖誕佳節及新年

Dear WCAC Members,

As you may know, the Omicron variant of Covid-19 has quickly become the dominant strain of Covid in Ontario. WCAC is issuing an advisory to remind everyone to maintain caution regarding protecting yourself and your family and preventing the spread of the virus.

Facts about the Omicron variant (according to US CDC)

  • Easier to spread, potentially more transmittable than Delta Variant.
  • More break through infections
  • Top five symptoms are:
    1. Runny nose
    2. Headache
    3. Fatigue (either mild or severe)
    4. Sneezing
    5. Sore throat

Recommendations for all WCAC attendees:

  • Please continue to practice all WCAC COVID safety protocols including wearing a mask, social distancing, and checking for symptoms.
  • If you have any out of town visitors (including family members) visiting during the holiday season, please pay extra attention to the above symptoms. If anyone in your household displays the above symptoms, please stay home for Sunday Service. Online Service is available on WCAC.CA for these families.
  • Please pay extra attention to the provincial gathering rules when meeting friends & family during the holiday season.
  • After service, please fellowship outside and maintain social distancing

We wish you a Merry Christmas and Blessed New Year

溫宣緊急應變小組醫療組的溫馨建議：

    ★ 基本原則如下:

  1. 與人保持安全距離，必要時戴口罩以防口沫分濺。
  2. 勤用肥皂洗手，如果人在外無法做到，要用含60-70%酒精濃度的乾洗手液洗手。如果都做不到，用面紙擦拭並立即丟棄。
  3. 為防止病毒侵入，避面用手接觸臉部尤其是口，鼻及眼睛。

Advice from our Medical Team

    ★ The Basic Principles are

  1. Prevent aerosal spread by social distancing +/- face mask
  2. Keep hands clean by washing. If not possible, use 60-70% alcohol based hand sanitizer. If can't do both, use a tissue and dispose immediately
  3. Do not let microbes get access into our bodies. Therefore do not touch the face, especially eyes, nose and mouth.
